About Marc and Indonesia

Marc was raised in utah and grew up in a middle class neighborhood. He is a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day saints. When he was 18 years old he served a mission for the church where he spent two years teaching the gospel of Jesus Christ in Indonesia. Now he is attending BYU and spends his days doing homework from morning until night with no rest or free time.

Indonesia is most know for its tourist attractions like the Island of Bali, the many religious temples, and the zoo's. There are many things about indonesia that people do not know.

The value of one dollar in indonesia is {{indorate}} Rupia


This does not mean much when you do not know how much items cost in Indonesia, so to put it in perspective you can buy a cheap meal ranging from 6,000-15,000 Rupia. Meaning you can get a meal for about a dollar. Nicer meals cost anywhere from 20,000 to 100,000. The most expensive meal I got was in a 5 star hotel and was a buffet for 200,000 Rupia or just under 15 Dollars. These prices are for the main islands not including Bali.
